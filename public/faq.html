Why should I limit the number of times I can modify my nickname?

For security reasons of your transaction, it is convenient for user identification. At present, we restrict the nickname to be modified only once.

Why is real-name authentication required?

For the safety of your funds, we restrict the association of your receiving account with the real-name information of the current account.

Why does the converted amount in assets change?

The equivalent calculation in the asset is the value of the current holding digital currency equivalent to the U.S. dollar, which changes due to fluctuations in the price of the digital asset. The number of your digital assets has not changed.

What is frozen assets?

Frozen assets means that when you conduct transactions or withdrawal operations, the process is not fully completed, and the current assets are temporarily under the custody of the system and cannot be freely controlled by you. It does not mean that you have lost the asset or the asset is abnormal, please rest assured

What should I do if I forget the fund password?

If you forget your fund password, please go to "My-Settings-Click Set Fund Password" to reset it.
