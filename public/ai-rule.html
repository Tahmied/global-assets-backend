Investment Rules

Income Calculation

Members deposit 10,000U in the platform, choose a financial product with a period of 6 days and a daily return rate of 0.3% ~ 0.5%. The daily return is as follows:

  • Minimum Value: 10,000U × 0.3% = 30U
  • Maximum Value: 10,000U × 0.5% = 50U

Therefore, after 6 days, you can earn an income of 210U - 350U. Income is issued daily and can be accessed at any time. The deposited principal will be automatically returned to your wallet account after the product expires.

Liquidated Damages

If you wish to withdraw the unmatured principal early, a penalty of 50% of the invested amount will be incurred.

Example:

  • Investment Amount: 1,000U
  • Liquidated Damages: 50% × 1,000 = 500U
  • Actual Refund: 1,000U − 500U = 500U
